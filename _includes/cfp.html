Call for Proposals

The event's call for proposals is available on on EasyChair. The CFP will be open until 2018/04/16. Please submit your talks as soon as possible!

When submitting talks, please identify if you are submitting a talk for the HBase track or the Phoenix track. All talks submitted for the HBase track will be reviewed by select members of the Apache HBase project management committee (PMC). The same will be done for talks submitted to the Phoenix track and members of the Apache Phoenix PMC.
